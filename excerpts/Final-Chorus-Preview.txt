Oh sohniye tera nasha going straight to my head,
Mainu pagal banaunda ae the words that you said,
Tera roop ae nasheela like a dangerous game,
Main sarhda haan andar driving me insane,
Asmaan de tareyan nu watching them fall ni,
Main fadh launga hath tera answering the call ni,
Sadi rooh da connection forever combined ae,
Main tera he rahunga till the end of time ae.
