Kaale libaas vich aundi ae looking so fine,
Pura shehar ruk janda freezing the time,
Main kalla khada haan waiting for you ni,
Dark shadows de vich feeling so true ni,
Tere hasse de pichhe hidden pain disdi,
Meri jaan kadd lendi magic jad kardi,
Ikk wari takk lai tu straight in my eyes ni,
Tere pichhe main rulda haan crossing the lines ni.
