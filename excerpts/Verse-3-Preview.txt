Wafadari mere khoon vich loyalty brand ae,
Main kalla ladd launga making my stand ae,
Tere bin mere haase turning to dust ni,
Main pathar ban gaya haan broken the trust ni,
Kaale badlan chon digdi heavy rain drop ae,
Main rukna ni kade going straight to the top ae,
Hanju lukoke main hard face rakhda,
Teri khushi di khatir pure pain chakhda.
